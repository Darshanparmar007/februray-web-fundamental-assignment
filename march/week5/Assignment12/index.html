A dog is known as man’s best friend because of the way dogs are loyal and friendly to their masters. Just like every dog owner, children love dogs more than anything in the world. It’s not just about children, anyone with a pure heart simply can not deny that they love dogs, unless they are actually allergic to dogs. For the families that have dogs, they are not just pet animals, but a true member of their family. Through this essay on My Pet Dog, we will be talking about all the adorable and lovely things that dogs do.
